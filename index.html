DIY rocket fuel

rocket fuel, REAL rocket fuel, not the water bottle ones with bike pumps, no, real rocket fuel that ignites and flys hundreds of feet into tha air!

you will need: 1/2 cup of potassium nitrate (can be found in stump remover, most garden shops will sell it), 1/2 cup of white sugar, the more fine the powder, the better it will burn, this applies to the potassium nitrate too.

if the bottle of potassium nitrate is very old i recomend to bake it for 30 minutes WITHOUT the sugar, the potassium alone will only react like sugar, but if you combine both together the results will get... explosive

to increase the rocket power dramatically, cook the powder until it turns like dough, be aware that this rocket fuel is very dangrous and can let off a large amount of smoke

as you can see, make sure there are no air bubbles inside the fuel or else it will explode, in my case you can see that i poked a hol too far into the fuel and it came out the other end, the whole rocket should be tigtly sealed
